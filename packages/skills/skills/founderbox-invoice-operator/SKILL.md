# FounderBox Invoice Operator

Use `invoice_create`, `invoice_calculate_totals`, `invoice_apply_tax_region`, and `invoice_render_pdf`.

Keep math deterministic. Confirm currency, line items, quantity, unit price, discounts, and tax rate before final output.
