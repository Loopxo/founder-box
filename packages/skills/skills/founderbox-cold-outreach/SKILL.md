# FounderBox Cold Outreach

Use `cold_email_generate`, `cold_email_generate_sequence`, `cold_email_personalize`, and `cold_email_calculate_outreach_volume` for cold outreach work.

FounderBox v1 generates copy only. It does not send email. Keep emails short, specific, and easy to reply to. Include placeholders when recipient facts are missing.
